How to Switch Suppliers

Switching is free, takes about 5 minutes, and your utility still delivers the gas. Nothing changes except your supply rate.

Compare rates above. Fixed rates lock in your price. Look for low $/ccf with no early termination fee.

Sign Up Online

Click "View Offer" on any plan above. You'll need your utility account number from your bill. Takes ~5 minutes.

3

Done. That's It.

Your utility still delivers the gas and handles emergencies. You just pay less for the supply portion of your bill.
